Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)

STEM, which stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, is one of the most popular strands under the Academic Track in Senior High School in the Philippines. It is also offered as part of LCBA’s Senior High School (SHS) programs. STEM is perfect for students who want challenging subjects, future-ready skills, and careers in science and technology.

Best College Courses After STEM

STEM is the best choice if you want to take courses like


  • Engineering (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, etc.)
  • Medicine / Nursing / Pharmacy
  • Computer Science / Information Technology
  • Architecture
  • Biology, Chemistry, Physics
  • Mathematics and Statistics


This strand is designed for students who are interested in



  • Science experiments and discoveries
  • Technology and innovation
  • Engineering and design
  • Advanced mathematics and problem-solving

Future Careers for STEM Students

Graduates often become


  • Engineers
  • Doctors or healthcare professionals
  • Scientists or researchers
  • Architecture
  • Programmers and software developers
  • Architects
  • Data analysis

The Objectives of the STEM Strand in SHS

The STEM strand is a specialized program designed for students who are interested in studying Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) in college. Our STEM program for SHS offers comprehensive courses and hands-on projects that hone students’ analytical skills, preparing them for a future in science and technology.
